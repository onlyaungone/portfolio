Voltage Training Club (VTC App)

Category: Full Stack Fitness Tracking App

GitHub: link

A mobile-first fitness application built with React Native, Firebase, and AWS. Users can track workouts, log sets & reps, view exercise history, and follow training plans. The app supports real-time progress tracking, cloud sync, and secure authentication.

Developed for a modern, responsive experience across devices. Data is securely managed with Firestore. (Demo video available in repository.)
